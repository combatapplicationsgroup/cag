About us

Welcome to Combat Application Group

Our hardcore milsim community replicates the Combat Applications Group. Our mission is to give a authentic and not only immersive but also a unique experience.

To join Combat Application Group, prospective members undergo a rigorous training pipeline comprising Operator Training Course (OTC) is an intense and highly selective program designed to train and evaluate candidates for assignment to the Army's elite Delta Force, focusing on advanced tactics, marksmanship, and physical and mental endurance.
